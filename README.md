# GroupInn

## A Digital Home for Communities

GroupInn helps people discover, join, and grow meaningful groups around shared interest without needing to share phone numbers or social media handles.


_________________________  OVERVIEW  _________________________


GroupInn provides a simple, organized space where people can connect through groups.  
Each group acts as a small, private community where members can chat, share ideas, and build meaningful relationships.

The goal is to make group interactions easy, safe, and human â€” focusing on connection rather than complexity.


_________________________  WHY IT MATTERS  _________________________


People stay engaged with products when they find their people.  
It helps users form real, intentional connections that last.


_________________________  TARGET USERS  _________________________


GroupInn is built for anyone who thrives in communities or collaborates in groups, including:

- Clubs, cohorts, and interest groups  
- Learning and project teams  
- Online communities and social circles 
- Associations  


_________________________  CORE FEATURES  _________________________


**Group Discovery**  
Find and join communities that match your interests.

**Group Chat**  
Message your group in real time.

**Recognition**  
Highlight group milestones and contributions.

**Privacy Control**  
Connect with others without sharing personal details.



_________________________  ROADMAP  _________________________


### Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
1. Create and join groups  
2. Real-time group chat  
3. Member profiles  


### Future Enhancements
- File sharing  
- Voice channels
